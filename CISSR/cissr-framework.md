# CISSR Framework: Theoretical Foundation

## 1. The Three Pillars

### A. Material Self-Healing
- **Crystalline Healing**: Inspired by concrete admixtures (Kryton, Penetron) that grow crystals to seal cracks
- **Polymer Reformation**: Reversible hydrogen bonds that "stitch" damaged surfaces
- **Radiation Passivation**: Nanoparticles that form protective layers under neutron flux

### B. Biological Integration
- **Engineered Microbes**: Radiation-tolerant bacteria (e.g., *Deinococcus radiodurans*) modified to:
  - Precipitate minerals for crack sealing
  - Bioleach heavy metals from contaminated water
  - Produce biopolymers for structural reinforcement
- **Mycelial Networks**: Fungal growth models for:
  - Optimized fluid distribution (mimicking nutrient transport)
  - Self-organizing repair pathways

### C. Symbolic Control Logic
- **Sensor Fusion**: Thermal, acoustic, radiation, and structural sensors feeding into a symbolic AI
- **Geometric Translation**: Mapping biological rules (from BioGrid2.0) to binary control signals
- **Predictive Manifolds**: Machine learning models that anticipate failure modes before they occur

## 2. The Self-Healing Loop

```

[Damage Event] → [Sensor Detection] → [CISSR Core Decision]

[Trigger Response]
├── Chemical: Release healing agents (e.g., crystalline admixtures)
